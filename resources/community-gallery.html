temperature check: how do you feel about the climate?

climate anxiety

FUTURE-FACING

fear, stress, or worry stemming from a sense of impending climate change impacts and processes; a sense of threat & uncertainty about environmental doom. can be mental, emotional, physical

eco-grief

MOURNING

grief from experienced or anticipated ecological losses, including the loss of species, ecosystems and meaningful landscapes due to acute or chronic environmental change

solastalgia

IN-THE-PROCESS

distress that is produced by negatively perceived environmental change impacting on people while they are directly connected to their home environment

climate hope

PERSEVERING

the acknowledgement of the climate crisis, and the simultaneous focus on the power of collective action to create a sustainable future; the unyielding investment in individual and community agency to make a difference

Did you know? In a research project surveying 1,000 young people aged 16-25 years in Canada, 78% of youth expressed that climate change affects their overall mental health. 37% reported that their feelings about climate change negatively impacted daily functioning.
